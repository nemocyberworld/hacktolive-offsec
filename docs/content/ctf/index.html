CTF Platforms

Domain: CTF • Focus: Capture The Flag Platforms & Wargames • Last Updated: 2025-09-05

Summary

Capture The Flag (CTF) platforms are online environments that provide security challenges across categories like cryptography, web, reverse engineering, exploitation, and forensics. These platforms are ideal for building cybersecurity skills in a gamified format, either solo or in teams, and often simulate real-world offensive and defensive scenarios. Below is a curated list of active, free, and freemium CTF platforms.

Available Platforms

  • CTFTime – Global CTF event tracker and team ranking site.
  • CTF Komodo Security – CTF challenges hosted by Komodo Security.
  • Academy Hackaflag – Brazilian platform with guided CTF challenges.
  • EchoCTF – Persistent CTF environment with red/blue team practice.
  • CyberSecLabs – Vulnerable lab machines with a CTF-style platform.
  • CyberDefenders – Blue team CTF challenges focused on DFIR and SOC skills.
  • Root Me – Popular platform with 500+ challenges across multiple categories.
  • RingZer0 Team – CTF challenges with cryptography, web, and exploitation focus.
  • HackTheBox – Large CTF-style platform with vulnerable machines and labs.
  • TryHackMe – Guided learning paths with CTF-style rooms and challenges.
  • CTF365 – Persistent, online CTF environment with real-world scenarios.
  • Hacker101 CTF – Free web hacking challenges by HackerOne.
  • PicoCTF – Beginner-friendly CTF created by Carnegie Mellon University.
  • PicoGym – Always-available practice challenges from PicoCTF.
  • 247CTF – Online CTF with continuously running challenges.
  • HackThisSite – Classic web and application hacking missions.
  • HackThis!! – Long-standing site with web hacking challenges.
  • Enigma Group – Web security challenges and realistic missions.
  • Backdoor – Indian-origin platform with curated security challenges.
  • W3Challs – Platform offering diverse challenges with community leaderboard.
  • WeChall – Aggregator and leaderboard connecting multiple CTF platforms.
  • Newbie Contest – Platform targeting beginner CTF players with practical challenges.
  • Root in Jail – European-based online CTF challenges.
  • Pwn Adventure 3 – 3D MMORPG with security vulnerabilities to exploit.
  • CMD Challenge – Fun Linux terminal challenge game for shell practice.
  • OverTheWire – Classic wargames platform (Bandit, Narnia, Leviathan, etc.).
  • SANS Holiday Hack (CounterHack) – Annual themed CTF with story-driven security challenges.
  • Hacker Security CTF – Brazilian-hosted CTF environment.
  • HackSec CTF – Freemium CTF platform with web and crypto labs.
  • Hacking-Lab – International CTF and security training platform.
