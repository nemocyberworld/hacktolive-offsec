Common Network Ports — Field Cheat Sheet

Domain: Network • Focus: Service/Port Mapping for Recon & Triage • Last Updated: 2025-09-04

Summary

A concise, categorized catalog of common ports and their associated services / protocols for fast reconnaissance, triage, and hypothesis-driven scanning. Use it to select scan targets, tune firewall rules, prioritize detections, and recognize unusual exposure during attack surface reviews.

Steps

  1. Identify the environment (Internet, DMZ, internal) and scope of assets or ranges.
  2. Pick relevant categories below (e.g., Web & Proxy, File Sharing, DB) to focus port selections.
  3. Run targeted scans and service validation (banner grabs, TLS checks, versioning).
  4. Interpret exposure vs. policy, document deviations, and remediate or harden where needed.
