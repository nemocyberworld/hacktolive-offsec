Reverse Engineering Labs

Domain: Reverse • Focus: RE, Binary Analysis & Pwnable Practice • Last Updated: 2025-09-05

Summary

Reverse engineering labs help you build practical skills in static/dynamic analysis, unpacking, deobfuscation, and understanding program behavior. The resources below are free (or freemium) and range from crackmes and embedded RE to full-on reversing CTFs. Use an isolated VM and follow legal/ ethical guidelines when practicing.

Available Labs

  • ROP Emporium – Purpose-built binaries that teach Return-Oriented Programming step-by-step.
  • Reversing.kr – Classic RE challenges across difficulty levels (PE tricks, crypto mishaps, packers).
  • Microcorruption – Browser debugger for embedded (MSP430) reversing and exploit puzzles.
  • FLARE-ON – Annual reversing CTF; past challenges archived for year-round practice.
  • pwnable.xyz – Always-on pwn/RE challenges with clean binaries and varied difficulty.
  • RingZer0 – Reverse Challenges – Focused reversing tasks: patching, crypto misuse, algorithm RE.
  • Alert To Win – Short, clever puzzles mixing RE/exploitation concepts.
  • crackmes.one – Massive community index of crackmes (Windows/macOS/Linux/Android/iOS) with ratings and tags.
  • Malware Unicorn – RE101/RE102 – Free reversing workshops with labs (x86 basics, tooling, unpacking).
  • OpenSecurityTraining2 (OST2) – University-grade free courses & labs (Intro to RE, Windows Internals, more).
  • Root Me – Reverse Engineering – Dedicated RE category with dozens of practice challenges (free; optional premium perks).
  • PicoGym – Reverse Engineering – CMU’s always-on practice set; great warm-ups and mid-level RE tasks.
  • Ghidra Training (Community) – Self-paced modules and exercises for Ghidra-based reversing.
  • pwn.college (RE track) – Structured lessons and auto-graded labs that include reversing fundamentals.
