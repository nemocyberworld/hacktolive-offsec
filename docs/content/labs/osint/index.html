OSINT Labs

Domain: OSINT • Focus: Open-Source Intelligence CTFs, VMs & Practice Challenges • Last Updated: 2025-09-05

Summary

Hands-on OSINT labs help you practice real investigation skills—finding, verifying, and analyzing public data—through CTFs, guided challenges, and pre-built virtual machines. Use these resources ethically and lawfully, and always work within a sandboxed environment when handling files or tooling.
