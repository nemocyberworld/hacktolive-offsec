Social Engineering Labs

Domain: Social Engineering • Focus: Ethical Phishing Simulation & Awareness Practice • Last Updated: 2025-09-05

Summary

This list highlights free (and freemium) resources for ethical social-engineering practice—primarily phishing simulation and awareness training in controlled, authorized environments. Use these only with explicit permission, in isolated test setups, and for education/defense. No operational guidance is provided here.

Available Labs

  • Gophish – Open-source phishing framework for running internal awareness simulations (free).
  • King Phisher – Open-source platform for authorized phishing campaigns and training (free).
  • Phishing Frenzy – Community project for managing consented phishing simulations (free, older but useful).
  • Social-Engineer Toolkit (SET) – Training toolkit for social-engineering scenarios in lab environments (free, offensive tooling—use ethically).
  • Social-Engineer.org – SE Labs & Framework – Free educational framework, playbooks, and exercises for ethical SE practice.
  • Google Phishing Quiz – Interactive quiz for recognition training; great for user awareness programs (free).
  • CyberDefenders (Email/Phish Analysis Challenges) – DFIR-style challenges that include phishing email investigation scenarios (free account).
  • Blue Team Labs Online (Phishing & SOC Labs) – Hands-on blue-team challenges including phishing triage and detection (freemium).
  • SANS Holiday Hack – Awareness & SE-adjacent challenges – Annual free CTF with social-engineering-flavored puzzles and storylines (free seasonal event).
